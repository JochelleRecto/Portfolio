Personal Summary

Jochelle is a queer visual artist and a graphic designer, currently a Multimedia Arts and Design student at iACADEMY, Makati. Joch uses her work to advocate for the queer community, women's rights and for those that experience the same issues as women in the country.

She finds pleasure in going through and experiencing passionate works in film, music, fine arts, and literature with a hot cup of black coffee. She values the importance of seeking other spaces and experiences for self-growth.
