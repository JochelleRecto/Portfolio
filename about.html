Personal Summary

Jochelle Recto is a visual artist and graphic designer currently pursuing her studies in Multimedia Arts and Design at iACADEMY, Makati. She co-owns Abakadapedia and acts as its Marketing Graphic Designer, a small business dedicated to providing innovative learning materials for kids.

Proficient in Graphic Design and Illustration with basic knowledge in 3D Modelling and Motion Graphics. Jochelle finds joy in exploring passionate works in Film, Music, Fine Arts, and Literature, often accompanied by a hot cup of black coffee. She values the importance of seeking other art and experiences to continually learn, evolve, and enrich her artistic journey.
